Welcome to NYCzine

Frank Vasquez was born and raised in New York City. His passion for technology and computers fostered his dream to work toward a career in IT consulting. In 1994, Frank graduated from the State University of New York at Albany with a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science. After graduating from college, he worked for several IT support and consulting firms. Fueled by his entre preneurial spirit and his desire to provide excellent personalized consulting services, Frank started Saber Technology, Inc. in 2000. Today, Saber Technology provides user support to over 50 clients in a wide-range of industries including, but not limited, to marketing firms, medical offices, restaurants, architecture firms, not-for profit and public relations firms.
